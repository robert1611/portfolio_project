REAL ESTATE AND SHORT TERM RENTALS

Real estate has always been a passion for me and I thoroughly enjoy remodeling houses. Not only from the standpoint of designing, but I also like to get my hands dirty doing much of the renovation work myself, and with the help of my wife Roselaine. My specialties include kitchen installations, usually IKEA, and wordworking ranging from floor refinishing, to custom door barn door construction, to installation of doors and windows.
It's fun preserving older houses on the verge of being torn down, and bringing new life to it by upgrading the guts and mechanical aspects of the home while preserving the original charm that cannot be replaced by cookie cutter modern construction. I always think about all the families who have lived in these places before and sometimes even find family heirlooms behind sheetrock or buried in a closet.
My wife and I host a dozen short-term rentals on popular platforms such as AirBnB and VRBO, but hope to leverage newly gained HTML / CSS experience to customer websites to tap undeveloped markets and reach different audiences. Hosting comes with the good and the bad, but it's really enjoyable to meet new people and share your passion with them.

Born and bred New Yorker living in Houston, Texas. Excited to begin a career in programming and data science.
